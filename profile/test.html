Nicklas 'MiNiWolF' Pingel

Computer science theories and practice is a great interest of mine. Using this I have spark an interest in game development which I will continue to pursuit. I use my time to develop things I find interesting.

Through my time I have developed a few minor projects both in datastructures and game ideas. But has never done anything particularly interesting to me. However I have started working on a game world that I will continue to share when I do updates to the game along with comments and technology to follow along.

My great interest in fantasy and gaming in general is something I hope to be able to combine with my computer science background. I want to develop something I find fun and interesting while also giving something that others might enjoy.
